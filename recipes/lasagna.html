Mexican Lasagna

This is a great recipe for making the most delicious mexican lasagana. Italian contruction of pasta and sauce meets the spices of mexico! Delicious!

Ingredients

  • 2 Pounds ground beef.
  • One 16oz can refried beans.
  • One 4oz can chopped green chilies.
  • 2 Pounds ground beef.
  • One 16 ounce can refried beans.
  • One 4 ounce can chopped green chilies.
  • 1 Envelope taco seasoning.
  • 2 Tablespoons hot salsa.
  • 12 Ounces uncooked lasagna noodles.
  • 4 Cups shredded Mexican or Colby Jack cheese.
  • One 16 ounce jar mild salsa.
  • 2 Cups water.
  • 2 Cups sour cream or plain Greek yogurt.
  • One 2.25 ounce can sliced black olives drained, optional.
  • Cilantro, diced tomatoes, green onions optional.

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Brown the ground beef and drain the fat.
  3. Stir in the refried beans, green chilies, taco seasoning and hot salsa.
  4. In a greased 9x13 pan, layer 1/3 of the lasagna noodles, 1/3 of the beef and bean mixture and 1 cup of cheese.
  5. Repeat two more times.
  6. In a medium sized bowl, combine the salsa and water. Pour over the lasagna.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 1 hour.
  8. Remove the foil and top with sour cream (or Greek yogurt), olives and remaining cheese.
  9. Bake an additional 5 minutes. Remove from the oven and let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
  10. Top with diced tomatoes, diced green onion and cilantro, if desired.
